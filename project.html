Project veilige samenleving

(Project save society). We had to do one of the following projects: A blender animation, a project with the micro:bit, make a robot, make a serious game or a website. It had to be themed around making society saver. Me and my team choose a combination of the micro:bit and the robot (robotica). It needed to be able to follow lines with curves lines and gapes in it. we used the micro:bit as a sensor, it measured the temprature, the humidity, sCO2 and eTVOC. Then it would send the information to another micro:bit which would display the information and warn you when they where to high or to low.
